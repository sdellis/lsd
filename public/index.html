“Case” Studies

The LSD has been designed to meet a number of specific purposes (needs?). First of all, it saves space. Second, it's highly versatile: use it live, use it acoustic - either way, the sound is great. It's great for drummers on the go, or one-man-bands (All parts fit in the suitcase). It won't keep your neighbors up at night. It is eco-friendly and recycles stuff that people throw out. And finally, it just looks cool. People will stop to check you out. In short, the LSD puts a little kick in your step!
